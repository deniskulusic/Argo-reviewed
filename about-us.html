A Class of Its Own

Argo represents a singular achievement in the Adriatic, a vessel that transcends the traditional boundaries of yachting to offer an experience that is simply nowhere else to be found.

The Argo Experience

Scale, Service, and Freedom

At 55 meters and spanning five meticulously designed decks, Argo is not merely a superyacht; it is a benchmark of extraordinary scale and refined sophistication, envisioned for those who seek the absolute pinnacle of coastal exploration.

What defines the Argo experience is the seamless harmony between expansive space and intimate service. While our physical presence is monumental, the atmosphere on board remains profoundly personal. With a dedicated crew of fifteen for just twenty-six guests, we provide a level of intuitive care where every desire is anticipated before it is even voiced.

We hold the conviction that true prestige is found in the absolute freedom of the individual. It is the liberty to curate a bespoke narrative, a sequence of days defined not by a schedule, but by the spontaneous pursuit of whatever brings you peace or exhilaration.
