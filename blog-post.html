Retreat Yachting experience is based in southern Croatia, the most beautiful part of the Adriatic Sea. There is no better way to experience the land of a thousand islands, so we invite you to take this ride with us. The Body: Our wellness cruises offer a variety of physical activities, from yoga and Pilates sessions on deck to guided hikes on secluded islands. Guests can also indulge in spa treatments, massages, and holistic therapies designed to rejuvenate the body and mind.

Our culinary offerings focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients, with menus crafted to nourish the body and delight the senses. Guests can expect a blend of Mediterranean flavors and wellness-focused dishes that support their health and well-being.
