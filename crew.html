THE TEAM

The Architects of Your Unforgettable Journey

Captain

Marin Sovulj

At the helm of Argo is Captain Marin Sovulj, a native of Split whose maritime journey spans two decades. His profound experience, honed through commanding large-scale cruises across Croatia, ensures every voyage is executed with masterful precision and an intimate understanding of the Adriatic’s nuances. Captain Sovulj’s leadership is characterized by an unwavering dedication to guest satisfaction and the seamless operation of the vessel, fostering an atmosphere of absolute confidence and serene prestige. His passion for excellence extends beyond the sea, reflected in his personal pursuits, which include a distinguished involvement in car racing, embodying a spirit of precision and controlled power.

First Officer

Lucijan Slavic

First Officer Lucijan Slavic, a distinguished native of Split, brings a dynamic blend of youthful vigor and seasoned expertise to Argo. With an extensive background as a skipper, his proficiency extends to the meticulous handling of tenders ranging from 7 to 20 meters, ensuring seamless transfers and exhilarating water-based excursions. His comprehensive understanding of maritime operations, coupled with his exceptional communication skills and adherence to international safety standards (ISM, ISPS), underscores his commitment to both the safety and enjoyment of every guest. Lucijan’s adaptable nature and proactive approach contribute significantly to the vessel’s harmonious environment, making him an invaluable asset to the Argo experience.

Chief Engineer

Branimir Ujevic

Chief Engineer Branimir Ujevic is the steadfast guardian of Argo’s sophisticated systems, bringing over two decades of unparalleled expertise to the vessel. His extensive career, marked by service on exquisite charter yachts and ice-class ships up to 60 meters, speaks to a profound technical mastery. Branimir’s approach is characterized by a proactive foresight and a meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every aspect of Argo’s engineering operates with flawless efficiency and reliability. His dedication to safety and operational excellence underpins the serene and uninterrupted comfort enjoyed by all aboard, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the Adriatic experience with complete peace of mind.

Engineer

Engineer Josip Bozic contributes a wealth of practical experience and a keen eye for detail to Argo’s technical operations. With a background encompassing charter sailboats, cruising ships, and extensive deckhand duties, Josip possesses a comprehensive understanding of vessel maintenance, tender operations, and watch-standing. His ongoing pursuit of further education as a Ship Engineer underscores his dedication to continuous improvement and operational excellence. Josip’s diligent approach ensures that all systems function flawlessly, contributing to the seamless and elevated experience aboard Argo.

Chief Stewardess

Ana Milos

Chief Stewardess Ana Milos orchestrates the impeccable interior experience aboard Argo with a blend of sharp operational acumen and intuitive grace. Her rapid ascent within the yachting industry, culminating in her role as Chief Stewardess within a year, is a testament to her exceptional leadership and discerning eye for detail. Having managed demanding itineraries on yachts up to 110 meters, Ana ensures every guest interaction is marked by discretion, precision, and a warm, anticipatory service. Her commitment to fostering a harmonious environment extends to mentoring her junior crew, ensuring a consistently superior five-star experience. Beyond her professional dedication, Ana finds balance and focus through her passion for volunteering with abandoned animals and practicing Pilates.

Chef

Ivan Ivcevic

Chef Ivan Ivcevic presides over Argo’s culinary realm, offering a gastronomic journey that spans the Mediterranean, Japanese, Fusion, and Middle Eastern cuisines, alongside classic Italian and French. With a distinguished culinary degree and a repertoire that includes specialized seafood preparations, Chef Ivan crafts bespoke menus tailored to individual preferences and dietary requirements. His award-winning expertise, recognized by prestigious institutions such as Villeroy & Boch, ensures that every meal is an exquisite event. Chef Ivan’s commitment to utilizing locally sourced, fresh ingredients, combined with his leadership in the galley, elevates dining aboard Argo to an art form, creating unforgettable culinary narratives for every guest.

Sous Chef

Jordana Vukovic

Sous Chef Jordana Vukovic brings a rich tapestry of maritime tradition and culinary passion to Argo’s galley. Hailing from Jesenice, a coastal village steeped in seafaring heritage, Jordana’s connection to the sea runs deep, influenced by generations of mariners. After a distinguished career in media, she returned to her true calling, refining her culinary skills at renowned establishments like Falkensteiner. Her expertise, honed through experience on MV Navigator, allows her to curate delightful menus that blend creativity with warmth. Jordana’s dedication ensures that every dish prepared aboard Argo is a testament to fresh, seasonal ingredients and a heartfelt commitment to gastronomic excellence.
