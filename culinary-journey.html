Experiences Dining 2min read

a gastronomic narrative

Every meal is an unscripted journey, where the only fixed element is your personal preference.

The menu serves as a blank canvas, curated daily to reflect your individual desires and the morning’s finest catch. Whether it is the salt-bright clarity of fresh tuna tartare and raw Adriatic scampi enjoyed in the open-air setting of the Upper Deck, or the sophisticated depth of a black truffle Bistecca alla Fiorentina served in the candlelit intimacy of the main salon, the experience is entirely yours to define.

Our chefs source with a relentless focus on provenance, line-caught seabass, Hvar fisherman’s stew, and sun-ripened produce from hidden island gardens, paired with the rarest regional vintages. From a sashimi tasting at sunset to a grand gala dinner concluded with a rose liqueur Rožada, the service is intuitive and the execution flawless. Aboard Argo, dining is not merely a service; it is a profound reflection of your own taste, served against the rhythmic backdrop of the Mediterranean.
