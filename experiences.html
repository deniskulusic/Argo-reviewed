Experiences Discover 5min read

a legacy of moments

explore the premier luxury yacht charter in Croatia. From bespoke dining to wellness and adventure, discover the ultimate Adriatic experience aboard Argo.

1. gastronomic narrative

Every meal is an unscripted journey, where the only fixed element is your personal preference. The menu serves as a blank canvas, curated daily to reflect your individual desires and the morning’s finest catch. Whether it is the salt-bright clarity of fresh tuna tartare and raw Adriatic scampi enjoyed in the open-air setting of the Upper Deck, or the sophisticated depth of a black truffle Bistecca alla Fiorentina served in the candlelit intimacy of the main salon, the experience is entirely yours to define.

Our chefs source with a relentless focus on provenance, line-caught seabass, Hvar fisherman’s stew, and sun-ripened produce from hidden island gardens, paired with the rarest regional vintages. From a sashimi tasting at sunset to a grand gala dinner concluded with a rose liqueur Rožada, the service is intuitive and the execution flawless. Aboard Argo, dining is not merely a service; it is a profound reflection of your own taste, served against the rhythmic backdrop of the Mediterranean.



2. the wellness sanctuary

Restoration on this scale is a departure from the expected, a realm where wellbeing is never standardized, only understood. It is a philosophy rooted in the belief that every rhythm is unique, and every body requires its own language of care. Here, the journey begins not with a menu, but with a conversation. A private consultation ensures that every movement and every touch is an intuitive response to your state of being in that singular moment.

Whether it is the targeted precision of a restorative facial ritual or the deep, rhythmic release of a tailored massage, the experience evolves in harmony with the sea. Guests may enjoy treatments on the open-air decks, where the expansive Adriatic vistas and the soft Mediterranean breeze elevate the sense of recovery. Upon the Sun Deck or the tranquil Beach Platform, Yoga and Pilates become personal ceremonies of vitality, while our light-filled gym offers a dedicated space for high-performance training. Removed from structure and obligation, it is a sanctuary of absolute discretion, providing the space for a profound, unhurried recovery that belongs entirely to you.

3. the rhythms of life aboard

The Adriatic becomes your private estate, offering a study in the art of the moment. For the adventurous, the sea is an endless invitation, from the high-velocity precision of Fliteboards and Seabobs to the spirited joy of the 21-foot upper-deck slide. Whether practicing your swing from the sunset golf tee or exploring the crystalline depths, every pursuit is defined by absolute freedom.

As evening descends, the panoramic deck is reimagined as an intimate celestial sanctuary. Our signature open-air cinema offers the rare luxury of film under a vast canopy of stars, where the only soundtrack is the gentle respiration of the Mediterranean. For those wishing to elevate the night’s atmosphere, the voyage can be further enriched by the vibrant energy of a private DJ or the haunting harmonies of traditional Dalmatian Klapa. Perhaps most evocative is the soulful resonance of a solo saxophonist, whose melodies drift across the deck to harmonize perfectly with the rhythmic pulse of the light waves. To deepen the connection to the land, a curated sommelier encounter with rare Croatian vintages and artisanal olive oils may be arranged, transforming the evening into a profound celebration of coastal heritage.



4. moments of discovery

Argo Privileged Access serves as a sophisticated concierge to the Adriatic, offering a realm of possibilities that extend the yacht’s intuitive service to the shore. Land-based discovery is entirely a matter of personal choice, perhaps a breathtaking arrival by private tender beneath the monumental limestone walls of Dubrovnik, or the Roman grandeur of Split, offering a commanding perspective of history from the water. Every transition is handled with an absolute removal of effort, allowing for unhurried sunset walks through Venetian Hvar or the medieval silence of Korčula’s walled interior.

The journey can be further elevated through curated encounters tailored to your curiosity, from guided hikes reaching the historic Blaca Hermitage to exploring the cascading vitality of Krka’s waterfalls. Whether discovering the tranquil saltwater lakes of Mljet or the vibrant pulse of Venetian Hvar, every detail is managed with the same discretion found on board. These land-based explorations are available as seamless enhancements to your journey, providing a refined bridge between the absolute privacy of the vessel and the timeless soul of the Croatian coast.



5. the family collective

For families traveling with children, Argo offers a realm of effortless flexibility where every generation’s peace of mind is prioritized. Guests may choose to engage our professional childcare specialists for the duration of the voyage, a single day, or even a solitary afternoon, providing parents the freedom to embrace moments of unhurried repose. These highly qualified professionals are accustomed to the nuances of international travel, ensuring that our youngest guests are safe, engaged, and genuinely cared for in an environment of absolute discretion.

This service is thoughtfully adapted to the unique rhythm of your family, allowing for a seamless transition between shared celebration and private time. Whether you require a dedicated presence for the week or occasional support to enjoy a quiet dinner ashore, every detail is managed with the same refined attention found throughout the vessel. Available as an intuitive enhancement to your journey, our childcare services ensure that the Argo experience remains as rewarding for parents as it is for the children in their care.
