moments of discovery

Argo Privileged Access serves as a sophisticated concierge to the Adriatic, offering a realm of possibilities that extend the yacht’s intuitive service to the shore.

Land-based discovery is entirely a matter of personal choice, perhaps a breathtaking arrival by private tender beneath the monumental limestone walls of Dubrovnik, or the Roman grandeur of Split, offering a commanding perspective of history from the water. Every transition is handled with an absolute removal of effort, allowing for unhurried sunset walks through Venetian Hvar or the medieval silence of Korčula’s walled interior.

The journey can be further elevated through curated encounters tailored to your curiosity, from guided hikes reaching the historic Blaca Hermitage to exploring the cascading vitality of Krka’s waterfalls. Whether discovering the tranquil saltwater lakes of Mljet or the vibrant pulse of Venetian Hvar, every detail is managed with the same discretion found on board. These land-based explorations are available as seamless enhancements to your journey, providing a refined bridge between the absolute privacy of the vessel and the timeless soul of the Croatian coast.
