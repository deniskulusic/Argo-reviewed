the family collective

For families traveling with children, Argo offers a realm of effortless flexibility where every generation’s peace of mind is prioritized.

Guests may choose to engage our professional childcare specialists for the duration of the voyage, a single day, or even a solitary afternoon, providing parents the freedom to embrace moments of unhurried repose. These highly qualified professionals are accustomed to the nuances of international travel, ensuring that our youngest guests are safe, engaged, and genuinely cared for in an environment of absolute discretion.

This service is thoughtfully adapted to the unique rhythm of your family, allowing for a seamless transition between shared celebration and private time. Whether you require a dedicated presence for the week or occasional support to enjoy a quiet dinner ashore, every detail is managed with the same refined attention found throughout the vessel. Available as an intuitive enhancement to your journey, our childcare services ensure that the Argo experience remains as rewarding for parents as it is for the children in their care.
