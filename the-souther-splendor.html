your 8-day escape

SPLIT TO DUBROVNIK

day 1: split to milna, brač

Your journey begins in the historic heart of Split. After a warm welcome aboard, we glide toward the sheltered harbor of Milna on the island of Brač. Renowned for its deep, sapphire bay and baroque architecture, this stone-carved village provides a tranquil and elegant introduction to the island life.

day 2: the pakleni islands, hvar

Wake to the scent of the sea as we cruise toward the Pakleni archipelago. This emerald chain of islands offers a sanctuary of secluded lagoons and pristine beaches. Spend the day at anchor, enjoying the water toys or a private lunch on deck, while the vibrant energy and historic charm of Hvar Town remain just a short, scenic tender ride away.

day 3: korčula

The “Little Dubrovnik” awaits. We dock at the fortified town of Korčula, a medieval jewel of narrow stone alleys and terracotta roofs. Explore the fabled birthplace of Marco Polo and enjoy an evening of fine dining within the ancient city walls.

day 4: lastovo

Escape to the remote beauty of Lastovo, a protected nature park. This island remains one of the Adriatic’s most authentic secrets, where the pace of life slows to the rhythm of the tides. Its star-filled skies and crystal-clear bays offer a rare sense of profound peace.

day 5: mljet

Discover the mystical allure of Mljet, the Adriatic’s greenest island. Wander through the lush national park, where saltwater lakes and a 12th-century monastery on a tiny islet create an atmosphere of ethereal beauty.

day 6: šipan

Part of the Elaphiti Islands, Šipan is a retreat of Renaissance villas and olive groves. It is the perfect location for a quiet afternoon of exploration or a final celebratory dinner in a secluded bay, surrounded by the scent of citrus and sea salt.

day 7: dubrovnik

The “Pearl of the Adriatic” reveals itself as we approach the monumental walls of Dubrovnik. Walk the ramparts as the city glows in the twilight, reflecting on a week of discovery before your final night aboard the Argo.

day 8: farewell

A final breakfast on deck as we prepare for disembarkation in Dubrovnik, leaving you with memories of a journey etched in light and water.
